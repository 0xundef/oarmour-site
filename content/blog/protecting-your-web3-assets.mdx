# Protecting Your Web3 Assets

In the rapidly evolving world of Web3, security is paramount. With the rise of decentralized finance (DeFi) and NFTs, the responsibility of safeguarding assets falls entirely on the user.

## Common Threats

1.  **Phishing Attacks**: Malicious websites that look identical to legitimate ones.
2.  **Malicious Smart Contracts**: Contracts designed to drain your wallet upon interaction.
3.  **Social Engineering**: Scammers posing as support staff to steal your private keys.
